# Privacy Policy for FoundIt

**Effective Date:** May 7, 2026
**Last Updated:** May 7, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the FoundIt mobile application ("FoundIt", "the app", "we", "us", or "our") handles information when you use it. FoundIt is a personal, non-commercial project provided free of charge to its users.

## 1. About FoundIt

FoundIt is a personal hobby project developed and maintained by an independent individual developer ("the developer"). The app is offered as-is, without warranty of any kind. The app does not display advertisements, does not sell user data, and does not share user data with third parties for marketing purposes.

## 2. Information We Collect

FoundIt collects only the minimum information necessary to operate the app:

### 2.1 Account Information
When you sign in to FoundIt, we collect and process your **email address** through Google Sign-In (Firebase Authentication). This is used solely to:
- Authenticate you and identify your session
- Determine whether your email is on the app's authorized-user allowlist
- Associate found-item records with the user who created them

We do not collect your name, password, profile photo, phone number, address, contacts, location, or any other personal information through the app.

### 2.2 App Content
The app stores records that you (or other authorized users) create, which describe found items. These records may include:
- A description of the found item
- A monetary or item value, if entered
- Timestamps for when records are created or modified
- The email of the user who created the record

These records are stored in Google Firebase Firestore.

### 2.3 Information We Do Not Collect
FoundIt does **not** collect:
- Location data
- Contacts or address book entries
- Photos, camera input, or microphone input
- SMS, call logs, or device identifiers used for tracking
- Health, fitness, or financial information
- Browsing or web history
- Any data for advertising or analytics profiling

## 3. How We Use Information

The information collected is used only to:
- Provide core app functionality (creating, viewing, restoring, and deleting found-item records)
- Authenticate users and enforce the app's authorized-user list
- Maintain data integrity (e.g., soft-deleting and restoring records)

We do not use your information for advertising, profiling, or any commercial purpose.

## 4. How Information Is Shared

We do **not** sell, rent, trade, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing or commercial purposes. Information is shared only with:

- **Google Firebase** (Firebase Authentication and Cloud Firestore), which acts as our backend service provider. Firebase processes data according to Google's privacy and security practices. See: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy
- **Other authorized users of the app**, who can see found-item records stored in the shared database, since the app is designed for collaborative item tracking.
- **Legal compliance**, if required to do so by law, court order, or other valid legal process.

## 5. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on Google Firebase servers, which use industry-standard encryption in transit (TLS) and at rest. Access to the database is controlled by Firestore Security Rules.

While we take reasonable measures to protect your information, no method of electronic storage or transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. You use the app at your own risk.

## 6. Data Retention

- **Account data:** Retained while you have access to the app. If you would like your authentication record removed, contact the developer (see Section 11).
- **Found-item records:** Retained indefinitely so that the app's history remains available to authorized users. Deleted items are soft-deleted and may be restored by users with admin access.
- **Deletion requests:** You may request deletion of your account email and any records you created by contacting the developer.

## 7. Children's Privacy

FoundIt is not directed to children under the age of 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact the developer so it can be removed.

## 8. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction (for example, under the GDPR or CCPA), you may have rights to:
- Access the personal information we hold about you
- Request correction or deletion of your personal information

To exercise any of these rights, contact the developer at the address in Section 11.

## 9. Permissions Used by the App
