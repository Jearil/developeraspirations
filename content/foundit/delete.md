# Deleting Your FoundIt Account

If you would like to delete your FoundIt account, follow the instructions below.

## Request via Email

Account deletion is currently handled by contacting support directly. To request deletion of your account:

1. Send an email to **support@developeraspirations.com**
2. Use the subject line: **Account Deletion Request**
3. Include the email address associated with your FoundIt account in the body of the message

We will process your request and confirm deletion within a reasonable timeframe.

## What Gets Deleted

Upon a confirmed deletion request, the following will be removed:

- Your authentication record (the email address used to sign in)
- Found-item records you created that are not shared with or relied upon by other authorized users

Note that found-item records that are part of a shared database visible to other authorized users may be retained as part of the app's shared history.

## In-App Deletion

Support for self-serve account deletion directly within the FoundIt app is planned for an upcoming release. Once available, you will be able to delete your account from within the app without needing to contact support.

## Contact

For deletion requests or questions, email: **support@developeraspirations.com**
